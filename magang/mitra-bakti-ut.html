About Company

PT Mitra Bakti UT (MBUT) is a company engaged in Integrated Facility Management (IFM), focusing on providing integrated facility management services to support company operations across various industrial sectors. As part of the Yayasan Karya Bakti United Tractors (YKBUT) ecosystem, PT Mitra Bakti UT is committed to providing professional, safe, and high-quality services, prioritizing customer satisfaction and implementing an Occupational Safety and Health (K3) culture. The company was founded to meet the increasingly complex needs for business operational support services through effective, efficient, and sustainable solutions. PT Mitra Bakti UT's head office is located at Jl. Raya Bekasi KM 22, Cakung, East Jakarta. The company serves more than 50 customers is supported by approximately 1,500 professionals, and has operational reach in more than 40 service areas across Indonesia. This demonstrates the company's capacity to provide integrated services to various industrial sectors, including mining, construction, manufacturing, and offices. PT Mitra Bakti UT's core services include General Services & Support, Building Maintenance, Facility Maintenance, and Water and Waste Management. In addition, the company also provides mechanical and electrical (M&E) system maintenance services, building cleaning services, utility management, occupational safety, and various other operational support services. All of these services are designed to ensure customer facilities operate optimally, safely, and efficiently.
