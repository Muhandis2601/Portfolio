About the Company

PT PLN (Persero) UP3 Bekasi is a business unit of PT Perusahaan Listrik Negara (Persero), the state-owned electricity company responsible for supplying electrical power across Indonesia. The Bekasi unit — officially known as Unit Pelaksana Pelayanan Pelanggan (UP3) Bekasi — is responsible for managing electricity distribution, customer service, and network operations across the Bekasi service area. With a mission to provide reliable, affordable, and sustainable electricity, PT PLN plays a vital role in supporting the daily activities of households, businesses, and industries in the region.
