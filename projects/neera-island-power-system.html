Project Overview

About the Project

This project focused on the development and optimization of the electric power system in Neera Island, with particular emphasis on integrating solar energy into the existing electrical distribution infrastructure. The initiative aimed to deliver a reliable, cost-effective, and sustainable energy solution that leverages renewable energy sources to meet the island's growing energy demands.

A comprehensive 10-year master plan was developed for Neera Island's electric power system, covering generation, transmission, and distribution across the island. Solar power was integrated into the electrical distribution network as the primary renewable source, with a focus on long-term sustainability and reduced dependency on fossil fuels.

The project involved in-depth technical and economic analyses to ensure the feasibility, scalability, and reliability of the proposed power system. Key considerations included load forecasting, solar resource assessment, infrastructure sizing, and grid stability under varying demand conditions.
