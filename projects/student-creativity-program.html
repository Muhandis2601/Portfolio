Student Creativity Program 2023 – GAMATEA

Automatic Tea Leaf Cutter to Reduce the Risk of Low Back Pain

Sekolah Vokasi Universitas Gadjah Mada

About the Project

In 2023, this project was undertaken as part of the Student Creativity Program (PKM — Program Kreativitas Mahasiswa) under the title "GAMATEA: Implementation of Automatic Tea Leaf Cutter Device to Reduce the Risk of Low Back Pain Among Tea Leaf Pickers." As team leader, the project successfully secured funding from the Ministry of Education, Culture, Research, and Technology (Kemendikbudristek).

The funded project provided the necessary equipment to the partner, PT Pagilaran, located in Batang, to develop and implement the automatic tea leaf cutting device. The primary objective was to mitigate the risk of occupational low back pain among tea leaf pickers by automating the manual cutting process, thereby improving worker well-being and productivity.

This project demanded strong skills in proposal writing, creative problem-solving, project management, and cross-functional coordination. Successfully passing the national funding stage demonstrated both the merit of the innovation and the team's ability to deliver a compelling and technically sound proposal to a national review board.

Tools & Technologies

  • Proposal Writing — National PKM funding proposal development
  • Mechanical Design — Automatic leaf cutter mechanism design
  • Project Management — Team leadership and deliverable coordination

Key Outcomes

  • Successfully secured national funding from Kemendikbudristek for the GAMATEA project.
  • Led a multidisciplinary team as project leader from proposal development through implementation.
  • Delivered the automatic tea leaf cutter device to PT Pagilaran in Batang as project partner.
  • Addressed occupational health risks for tea leaf pickers through an innovative engineering solution.
