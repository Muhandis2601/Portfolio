3 Phase Inverter Circuit with 3 Phase Motor Arrangement

Perfect Sinusoidal Wave Output

Sekolah Vokasi Universitas Gadjah Mada

About the Project

This project was the final individual assignment for the UAS Electric Machine Control course at Sekolah Vokasi Universitas Gadjah Mada. It involved designing and implementing a 3-Phase Inverter Circuit with 3-Phase Motor Control that produces a perfect sinusoidal wave output.

Using Proteus for circuit simulation and Arduino IDE for programming the Arduino Uno microcontroller, the system was designed to ensure precise motor control with smooth sinusoidal output. The project provided in-depth exploration into motor control systems and inverter circuit design.

Throughout this project, valuable experience was gained in circuit design, simulation, and embedded programming. The hands-on nature of the work enabled the practical application of theoretical electrical engineering knowledge, fostering a deeper appreciation for the complexities of electrical machine control.

Tools & Technologies

  • Proteus — Circuit simulation and schematic design
  • Arduino IDE — Programming the Arduino Uno microcontroller
  • Arduino Uno — Hardware microcontroller for motor control logic

Key Outcomes

  • Successfully designed a 3-phase inverter circuit capable of driving a 3-phase motor with precise control.
  • Achieved a smooth and perfect sinusoidal wave output, validating the inverter design.
  • Deepened understanding of power electronics, signal waveform generation, and motor drive systems.
