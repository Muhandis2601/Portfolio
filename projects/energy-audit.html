Energy Audit

Institution Sekolah Vokasi UGM

Completion 100% Completed

Project Overview

About the Project

This semester 4 final project was carried out by a dedicated team of five, focusing on conducting a comprehensive energy audit at the Depok Sleman District Office. Using advanced measurement tools, the team assessed energy efficiency within the office environment, evaluating energy usage patterns and identifying concrete opportunities for optimization.

As the designated designer within the energy audit team, the role involved ensuring that all findings were meticulously documented and presented in a visually compelling format to facilitate clear communication of recommendations to the client. This required combining strong analytical skills with design proficiency to produce a professional audit report.

The project achieved a 100% audit completion rate and received positive feedback and enthusiasm from the Depok Sleman District Office for its thorough methodology and actionable energy-saving insights. This experience reinforced skills in project management, data analysis, and stakeholder communication, while deepening understanding of sustainable practices in organizational settings.
