Energy Audit

Energy Efficiency Assessment at Depok Sleman District Office

Sekolah Vokasi Universitas Gadjah Mada

About the Project

This semester 4 final project was carried out by a team of five, focusing on conducting a comprehensive energy audit at the Depok Sleman District Office. Using advanced measurement tools, the team assessed energy efficiency within the office environment from April to June 2024, evaluating energy usage patterns and identifying opportunities for optimization.

As the designated designer within the energy audit team, the role involved ensuring that all findings were meticulously documented and presented in a visually compelling format, facilitating clear communication of recommendations to the client. The audit achieved a 100% completion rate, reflecting the team's commitment and precision throughout the process.

The project received positive feedback and enthusiasm from the Depok Sleman District Office for its thorough audit methodology and actionable energy-saving insights. This experience reinforced skills in project management, data analysis, and stakeholder communication, while deepening understanding of sustainable practices in organizational settings.

Tools & Technologies

  • Energy Measurement Tools — Phase RST electrical measurement instruments
  • Gross Floor Area Measurement — Physical survey and space documentation
  • Electrical Equipment Inventory — Assessment and cataloguing of office electrical loads
  • Design Tools — Audit report layout and visual presentation

Key Outcomes

  • Achieved 100% audit completion rate across all designated areas of the Depok Sleman District Office.
  • Delivered actionable energy efficiency recommendations that received positive feedback from the client.
  • Gained hands-on experience in energy auditing techniques, electrical load inventorying, and phase measurement.
