Project Overview

About the Project

This project involved designing and developing electric power systems for the IKN (Ibu Kota Nusantara) area — Indonesia's new capital city — with a focus on identifying cost-effective solutions that balance budget constraints with optimal system performance.

A strategic master plan was developed incorporating both renewable energy sources and advanced distribution technologies. The design process combined technical rigor with economic considerations, ensuring that the proposed power system architecture could be realistically delivered within the budgetary parameters of a large-scale national infrastructure project.

By focusing simultaneously on technical performance and financial feasibility, the project produced cost-optimized solutions that demonstrate engineering competence and practical awareness of real-world project constraints. This experience enhanced the ability to develop sustainable, high-performance electrical systems in complex and budget-sensitive environments.
