Solar LED Light

Solar-Powered LED Lamp Design & Fabrication

Sekolah Vokasi Universitas Gadjah Mada

Period May 2023 – June 2023
Institution Sekolah Vokasi UGM
Category Electronics & PCB Design
Status Completed

Project Overview

About the Project

This project involved designing and building a solar-powered LED lamp as the final deliverable for the Technical Drawing 2 and Workshop Practice 2 courses. The project combined 3D modeling, electronic circuit design, and practical fabrication skills to produce a fully functional solar-charged lighting device.

Autodesk Inventor was used to design a detailed 3D model of the lamp casing, ensuring proper dimensions, structural integrity, and manufacturability. Eagle software was used to design the printed circuit board (PCB) for the electrical components, including the solar charging circuit, battery management system, and LED driver circuit.

The completed lamp is capable of recharging via a solar panel and also supports conventional cable charging as a backup option. This dual-mode charging system increases the lamp's practical usability across various environments. The project provided valuable hands-on experience in interdisciplinary engineering spanning mechanical design, PCB design, and sustainable energy application.
