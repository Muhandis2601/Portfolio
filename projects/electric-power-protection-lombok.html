Project Overview

About the Project

This final project was completed in collaboration with a colleague, focusing on developing a comprehensive and reliable electric power protection system for Lombok Island. The project encompassed designing protection subsystems for Grounding Indicators (GI) across 9 key substation locations, including Jeranjang, Ampenan, Mantang, Pulang Sengkol, Kuta, Selong, Pringgabayan, Bayan, and Tanjung.

Using ETAP software, the team engineered and simulated a comprehensive protection system to ensure optimal performance and reliability across all locations. The project placed emphasis on selecting the most suitable protection devices and relay coordination schemes, while also designing a robust electrical network that mitigates the risks of overvoltage and undervoltage through the use of capacitor banks on electrical buses.

This experience provided invaluable insights into protection system analysis and design, enhancing the ability to develop reliable and efficient electrical networks. It also reinforced the importance of teamwork and effective communication in tackling complex engineering challenges.
